Match Analytics

Three interactive explorers built from modeled tournament data. One evaluates how surprising each scoreline really was, one splits that surprise into luck versus market mispricing, and the last maps the trajectory of every shot to assess its quality.

Match Surprise Explorer

Every match plotted on two axes — how differently the game unfolded versus expectation (Δ₁) and how far the score defied the run of play (Δ₂). Filter by stage, highlight teams, click any point for the full breakdown.

Open the Results explorer
Luck & Mispricing Ledger

The same matches, decomposed. Splits each result's surprise into two axes the scoreline hides — luck (result beyond performance) and mispricing (performance the market underpriced). Rank teams or matches, click for the full breakdown.

Open the Ledger
Shot Explorer

Every shot in the tournament, twinned across two panels: where on the pitch it was taken and where it ended up in the goal mouth. Filter by stage, team, player, situation, outcome and xG band.

Open the Shots explorer
Save Explorer

The tournament from behind the gloves. Every on-target shot a keeper faced, mapped from strike point to goal-mouth placement, then each goalkeeper ranked on saves versus expectation — goals prevented above what xGOT says they should have conceded.

Open the Saves explorer
